A newly developed system to endorse a more powerful and efficient process of generating and editing information of current and future students has undergone various testings to further improve its implementation. If the end results are favorable, the system can be immediately installed in the university. Current tests are held in C-118 of the ICS Physical Science Building and are believed to be continued until 1st of June, 2016.

*Title of web app* was created and is continually maintained by CMSC100 students of UPLB for their final project. The students consisting of Guiao, Serato, Leonardo, Vallejo, Gabuya, Bilad, Gallenero, De Guzman, and Sofer put all their efforts in the materialization of this web application in hopes to improve the current student information retrieval system of the said university.
